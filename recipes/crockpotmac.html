Crock Pot Mac n Cheese

This is a simple but delicous mac n cheese recipie. Cook the noodles in a pot and mix all the ingredients into the crock pot. Then all you have to do is wait until its done coocking and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • elbow macaroni
  • salted butter
  • evaporated milk
  • half and half
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • white american cheese
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions until al dente (should still have a little 'bite' to it.)
  2. Spray a 4-quart slow cooker with nonstick cooking spray. Place the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker.
  3. Add the butter and stir until coated.
  4. Pour in evaporated milk, half & half, 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, cubed American cheese salt & pepper. Stir to blend.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 1 Â½ to 2 hours (gently stir occasionally as it cooks.)
  6. During the last 15 minutes of cooking, top with remaining cup of shredded cheddar cheese. Cover and allow cheese to melt.
  7. Once done, turn the slow cooker to the warm setting until ready to serve.
